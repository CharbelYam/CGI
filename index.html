Sheet GCI-A-302General information — about GCI

Built in Lebanon, for Lebanon.

GCI is a Lebanese renovation, fit-out, and construction firm working across tourism and hospitality, commercial, residential, wellness, and healthcare environments. Established in 2019, GCI has delivered more than 100 projects across Lebanon, working from its main office in Halat and sub office in Fatqa.

Mission. To deliver organized, high-quality renovation and fit-out works through dedicated site attention, timeline control, and accountable aftercare.

Vision. To become a trusted Lebanese partner for clients who want one team to manage the details from first consultation to long-term support.

Sheet GCI-A-400General notes — common questions

Before you request a quotation.

What kind of projects does GCI take on?

Renovation and fit-out are the main focus — upgrading commercial, residential, hospitality, and wellness spaces. Construction is a secondary strength for structural works, site preparation, or full builds when a project calls for it.

Where does GCI work?

Across Lebanon, from a main office in Halat and a sub office in Fatqa. Since 2019, GCI has delivered more than 100 projects spanning residential, commercial, hospitality, and wellness spaces.

How does a project start?

With a discovery and consultation meeting to understand your vision, requirements, budget, and timeline. You then receive a clear quotation and plan before any work begins.

Do you stay involved after handover?

Yes — aftercare is part of every project. GCI remains available for maintenance, adjustments, and ongoing support after completion.

What is the fastest way to get a quotation?

WhatsApp is the fastest: message +961 71 955 663 directly, or use the request form below and the team will follow up.

Client journey

A clear path from consultation to aftercare.

Every renovation, fit-out, or construction project follows a clear journey so clients understand the next step, the timeline, and the support available after handover.

N°01

Discovery & Consultation

We meet with the client to understand their vision, requirements, budget, timeline, and project objectives.

N°02

Design & Planning

We develop concepts, layouts, material selections, technical drawings, and coordinate all project requirements.

N°03

Build & Execution

Our team manages construction, fit-out, procurement, and on-site implementation while protecting quality and schedule.

N°04

Handover & Quality Assurance

The completed project is inspected, tested, and handed over so every detail meets the agreed standards.

N°05

Ongoing Support & Aftercare

We remain available after completion to address maintenance needs, adjustments, and continued client support.
